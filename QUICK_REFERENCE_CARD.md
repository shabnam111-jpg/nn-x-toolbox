## 🚀 Quick Start (5 minutes)

```
1. Open Aura AI Studio
2. Navigate to: Optical Intelligence → Palm Reading
3. Choose input: Photo, Camera, Video, or Live Stream
4. Click "Analyze" or scan your palm
5. Review 5 tabs: Reading, Features, Metrics, Guide, Data
6. Ask AI questions in Q&A section
7. Save or export results
```

## 📸 Best Photos Tips

**DO:**
- ✅ Use bright, natural light
- ✅ Flatten palm against camera
- ✅ Fill frame with hand
- ✅ Show entire palm surface
- ✅ Keep fingers relaxed

**DON'T:**
- ❌ Use shadows or dim light
- ❌ Angle or rotate palm
- ❌ Leave hand too small
- ❌ Hide fingers or lines
- ❌ Have glare or reflections

## ❓ Common Questions & Answers

**Q: Why is my quality low?**  
A: Improve lighting, flatten hand, fill frame, get closer

**Q: Can I retake a photo?**  
A: Yes! Choose same input source and replace

**Q: How do I save my reading?**  
A: Use "💾 Save Session" button (coming soon)

**Q: What if WebRTC doesn't work?**  
A: Use Camera Snapshot instead - simpler setup

**Q: Can I compare old readings?**

**Q: Is my data private?**

## 📊 What Each Line Means

### ❤️ Life Line
- Runs from thumb to pinky side
- Represents: Health, vitality, life events
- Length: NOT related to lifespan
- Breaks: Indicate major life changes

### 🧠 Head Line
- Horizontal across middle palm
- Represents: Intellect, learning style
- Slope: Shows emotional vs logical
- Clarity: Indicates clear thinking

### 💙 Heart Line
- Top horizontal line
- Represents: Emotions, relationships
- Depth: Shows emotional intensity
- Curves: Show how you express feelings
