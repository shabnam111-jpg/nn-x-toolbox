## 📸 Tips for Best Results

### **For Accurate Readings:**

1. **Lighting**
   - Use bright, natural light or LED lighting
   - Avoid shadows across your palm
   - Ensure consistent brightness

2. **Palm Positioning**
   - Flatten your palm against the camera
   - Show the entire palm surface
   - Keep fingers relaxed and slightly spread
   - Fill most of the frame with your hand

3. **Camera Settings**
   - Use high-resolution images if possible
   - Keep the camera steady
   - Maintain focus on the palm lines
   - Crop tightly around the hand

4. **Cleanliness**
   - Clean your hands before scanning
   - Dry completely to avoid glare
   - Ensure clear visibility of lines

### **If Scan Quality is Low:**
- 💡 Move closer to camera
- 💡 Improve lighting conditions
- 💡 Flatten palm more against camera
- 💡 Try Camera Snapshot instead of WebRTC
- 💡 Crop image to show just the palm

## 📚 Understanding Palmistry

### **The Three Major Lines:**

**❤️ Life Line**
- Starts between thumb and index finger
- Curves down toward wrist
- Represents: Vitality, energy, life events
- Length doesn't equal lifespan
- Breaks may indicate major life changes

**🧠 Head Line**
- Runs horizontally across middle palm
- Starts near thumb, extends toward edge
- Represents: Intellect, learning, thinking style
- Slope shows emotional vs. logical approach
- Clarity indicates clear thinking

**💙 Heart Line**
- Top horizontal line across palm
- Runs from little finger side to middle
- Represents: Emotions, relationships, love
- Depth shows emotional intensity
- Curves show relationship expressiveness

### **Secondary Indicators:**
- **Palm Shape**: Overall personality type
- **Line Depth**: Intensity of traits
- **Line Curvature**: Flexibility and adaptability
- **Intersections**: Influence points and life changes
- **Breaks/Islands**: Challenges and transitions
